Los Angeles Criminal Lawyers

The stigma of a criminal conviction can be professionally and personally debilitating but just being charged in and of itself carries significant consequence. You stand to lose your liberty as well as your dignity so it’s crucial that your lawyer has the expertise and the resources to defend against your charges.

The team at Roulston Urquhart Criminal Defence will fight diligently towards a successful result by tailoring our strategy to your case. Our team of lawyers have experience in every type of offence, and the skill to navigate the complexities of your case in particular.

Roulston Urquhart Criminal Defence maintains an office in Los Angeles for the convenience of our clients, and our lawyers pride themselves in being versed in the regional nuances. If you have been arrested in Los Angeles or suspect that criminal charges are pending, contact Roulston Urquhart Criminal Defence for a consultation. 

 

Roulston Urquhart Criminal Defence is a team of skilled criminal lawyers dedicated to smart, aggressive and strategic advocacy.
